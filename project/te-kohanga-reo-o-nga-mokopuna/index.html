Te Kōhanga Reo o ngā Mokopuna

  • Client
    Ministry of Education, Te Tāhuhu o Te Mātauranga
  • Maui role
    Graphic Design, Illustration
Te Kōhanga Reo o Ngā Mokopuna is a kaupapa Māori early childhood learning centre based in Wellington. Māui had the privilege of being able to design a series of illustrated tamariki characters, design a brand identity and finally design the layout for their Strategic Plan, Te Matiti o te Atea for 2019-2024. The visual theme was to instil a sense of new life and provide them with their own wholesome identity to promote their kōhanga as a place of learning, connection and future leaders.
